About WaveCore

WaveCore, founded in late 2025, is a company specializing in high-performance microwaves which blend cutting-edge technology with todays kitchen aesthetics. Our goal is to improve the cooking experience with user-friendly and energy-efficient appliances.

We have all microwaves for various types of use, including Over-The-Range and Countertop. We started this year in Sarnia, Ontario, by partners Nicole Roberts and John Doe, wanted to make and provide high-end quality microwaves for affordable prices.

They began with a small website online and quickly sold out and were able to expand. They have achieved local success in Sarnia and London.
