How does a microwave work?

Microwaves use electromagnetic waves to excite water molecules in food, which generates heat internally. As a result, food is cooked quickly and efficiently.

What products does WaveCore offer?

We offer a variety of microwaves including but not limited to:

  • Countertop
  • Over-The-Range
  • Built-In

Does Wavecore offer a warranty?

Yes! All microwaves automatically include a 1 year limited warranty, which covers parts and labour. Extended warranties are available for purchase.

Do you ship outside Canada?

Currently no, however we are looking to expand our company into the US soon!

Is microwave radiation harmful?

No, domestic microwaves are designed to contain raditation within the unit. As long as the door seal is intact, the microwave is safe.
